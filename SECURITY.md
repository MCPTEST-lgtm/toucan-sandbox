# Security Policy

We take security and privacy seriously. If you believe you have found a vulnerability in this repository, please follow the process below.

- Reporting: Please open a private report via GitHub Security Advisories or email security@example.com.
- Acknowledgement: We will acknowledge receipt within 2 business days.
- Triage: Issues are triaged within 5 business days to assess impact, scope, and exploitability.
- SLA targets (from acknowledgement):
  - Critical/High: initial fix or mitigation ETA within 7 days
  - Medium: ETA within 14 days
  - Low: ETA within 30 days
- Communications: We provide status updates at least weekly until resolution.
- Disclosure: We coordinate public disclosure once a fix or mitigation is available, or within 90 days by default.

Thank you for helping keep the ecosystem safe.
